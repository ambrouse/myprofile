# Gallery Folder Mode

Scene hien tai duoc doi sang che do "floating image space".

## Nguon anh
- Thu muc su dung: `gallery/`
- Manifest: `scene/image-manifest.js`

## Them anh moi
1. Copy anh vao thu muc `gallery/`
2. Them duong dan vao `scene/image-manifest.js`
3. Reload trang (`Ctrl + F5`)

## Tuong tac vat ly
- Chuot cham/keo qua anh: anh se bi day va xoay random.
- Chuyen slide: tao xung luc nhe day cac anh gan camera.
- Camera luon troi trong khong gian anh theo duong cong vo dinh.

## Mobile
- Tu dong giam so anh va dpr de uu tien do muot.
- Raycast tuong tac duoc throttle de tranh drop FPS.
