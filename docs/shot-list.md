# Shot List - Intro Cinematic (Phase A)

## Duration
- Target: 12-15 seconds before full interactive blend.

## Shot 01 - Establishing Orbit (0s-3s)
- Camera starts high and far, reveals layered platforms and central core.
- Purpose: communicate scale immediately.

## Shot 02 - Core Approach (3s-6s)
- Camera descends and arcs toward AI Core ring structures.
- Light pulses sync subtly with core motion.

## Shot 03 - Lateral Sweep (6s-9s)
- Camera slides sideways across pillars and bridge geometry.
- Purpose: showcase environment density and depth.

## Shot 04 - HUD Alignment (9s-12s)
- Camera settles to hero framing where UI overlay feels anchored.
- Blend into interactive control without abrupt jump.

## Interactive Handoff
- Cinematic influence fades over 1.2s.
- Pointer input gains full influence with spring smoothing.
- Slide navigation adjusts camera anchor per section zone.

## Technical Notes
- Use deterministic keyframes for camera anchors.
- Avoid high-frequency shake during text-heavy sections.
- Keep motion sickness risk low: limit max angular velocity.
